Blog Entry #6 Moter city "Detroit"

Today in class we watched the Chrysler commercial on their new Chrysler 200. The video was a speech on what Detroit is and shows where Chrysler comes from. The video was actually considered as one of the best commercials during the 2011 super bowl and just represents what the Motor City is. It begins with multiple dark views yet beautiful scenery of downtown Detroit and continues to show the elegance of what Detroit is. It continues by intensifying the mood of the commercial by including a song by a Detroit legend Eminem “Lose Yourself”. The song and the scene brings out the importance of the video extremely well but what really stands out was the voice over. The speech was inspirational and not only that it was so good that just hearing it once made audience really think about Detroit. Finally to end everything they include a tag “Imported From Detroit” which gave audience something remember, something to stick in there head. This video was amazing and may continue to be one of the best car shown on television so far.
