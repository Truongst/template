Blog Entry #2 World of warcraft

Today we watched the south park episode about World of Warcraft. The video focuses on kids who have no lives and spend multiple hours playing wow and they have one goal. To defeat a player that has been going around killing everyone. There are a lot of social issues from this episode, one is how kids the will play games and miss a lot of activities they could be doing. Also that they could get carpel tunnel from playing way to many games. The writer of the story, identifies a lot of the problems that come from playing a video games. He makes humiliating scenes to degrade the issues from playing too much games.
