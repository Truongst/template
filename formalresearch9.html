Conclusion

Radiology is an extremely new advancing career and will be important for the advancements in the human health field. With new technology emerging every year, radiology will continue to grow and many hospital will continue to utilize its versatility. Moreover there are many sub-specialties for students to take and there are also many jobs open. Students will need many conceptual skills and must understand what it takes to be a radiologist. Due to the massive growth the future of radiology will be a heavily desired career and will gain a lot of attention. Additionally radiology requires a lot of work and is a very mentally demanding job. Having the ability to look into humans without causing injury has been an amazing advancement and knowing that lives could be save will give hope to many people around the world.
