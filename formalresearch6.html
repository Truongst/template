Economics of Radiology

Radiology is an extremely quick growing field and finding a job after schooling would be simple. The reward in becoming a radiologist is extremely high and is one of the highest paying jobs in the medical field. According to HealthSalaries.com in 2013 the United States the average radiologist salary is about $216,577 a year yet is depends on the geographic region. Also experience plays a factor in the pay rate, usually every five years the annual salary will go up.
