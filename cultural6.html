Anticipated Effects

Within a few years after placing limits on video games it is possible to for society to get a lot better in general. Many players will be able to socialize and others and will be able to be a part of society. From encouraging people to stop playing video games will be help gamers escape the fake world that they live in. Players will be able to gain a new sense of identity. They will also allow grades of kids and teens to go up and reduce the number of anxiety attacks and other possible health issues. The results from stopping people from playing too much games can also reduce carpel tunnel syndrome and could actually save lives. For those who are currently effected by video games, it can help their will being to talk to a therapist and learn to participate in society. This interaction can brighten the mood behaviors of those players. In the end just playing games with a limit could benefit the lives of many and help some people reconstruct their lives.
