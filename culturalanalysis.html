  • Analysis of Artifact

    • Either standing near a powerful gladiator or next to a raccoon ninja there are all kinds of champions that will fight to the death in summoner’s rift! In the gaming world of League of Legends players will control these powerful characters and make them fight in order to reach the nexus and become victorious. There is all kinds of players that play league of legends, from athletes to kids to teachers to girls the variety is endless. It is currently the most popular online game in the world and has a huge impact on society. The video game puts five champions against five others and the team who can kill the enemy nexus (base) wins. League of Legends is a MOBA or multiplayer online battle arena which is a real-time strategy game that requires a lot of actions per minute (APM). Some players are able to reach almost 600 actions per minute, meaning they are able to click and tap buttons ten times in one second. The average player in league of legends can have around 200 APM (NBCNEWS2014). This requires a lot of quick finger movements that causes stress to the body and also causes a lot injuries. League of Legend also requires a lot of time to invest. Each game last for about forty five minutes and the entire time players are completely immersed into the game. Every second of the game counts of every moment matters so players can’t leave the screen for a single second and players can play multiple games every day. “Rank” play is a huge part of the system that allows player to place their skill against other players at that level. Rank games require a lot of time to climb the “ladder” and be placed at a better skill level (RiotGaming, 2013). Some people will invest all their time to try to climb and sometimes they may not even get anywhere. Online games have grown extremely fast and it has not only a game to few it is a reality. Some see games as a living, they see it as a better life, with no repercussions and no issues. When people look at league players they often see very unsocial people that will give up time with friends to play a game. League of legends depicts a lot of issues within the video gaming community.

  • Description of Issue

    • There are multiple issues to face within the video gaming community and addiction plays a major part. Video gaming addiction is recently a new disorder, created by the growing technology, has been classified by AMA or American Medical Association as a diagnostic and statistical mental disorder. According to Video-game-addiction.org nearly 10% of people who play video games exhibit addictive behavior towards video games and the same statistic is true about league of legends. A player name Chen Rong-yu recently died after playing league of legends for more than 23 hours in one period! He was found in a very popular video game café in Taipei and reports say that his arms were still stretched out with his hands on the keyboard and mouse (Mirrornews2012). As video games such as league of legends get better and better, more and more people will begin to face video game addiction. Some of the most common issues is carpel tunnel syndrome, which is a disease that effects the median nerve that runs from the arm to the wrist, which causes inflammation or may completely restrict movement of the fingers. It is usually caused from repeated movements of the joints or a lot of work pressure. Carpal tunnel syndrome is one of the most common injuries in professional gaming. Another big issue that League of Legend is the cause of many social issues for children or even college students. In a study in United States 89% of addicted video gamers are linked with anxiety, depression, and social phobia. Also they suffered lower grades and usually had difficulties escaping their problems (CNN 2011). After looking into the forums on league of legends there were a lot of stories of people that mention their lives were ruined by the game. One particular story was interesting (Link below). It was about a player that played League of Legends that would lie to his friends about playing games and played anyway. He also mentioned bad grades, and detachment from the outside world. League of Legends and the players that play video games face a major problem and video addiction will grow as technology develops.

  • Proposed Solution

    • In order to solve this issue many rules need to be put in place and areas to help show parents steps to fight video game addiction. Some will say that it is easy to cold turkey video games but it is not. Video games has been found to be more addictive than gambling and it can really harm a person to immediately quit. To propose a solution, it is best for League of Legends should put a number of hours a player can play in a period. Currently a person can play non-stop but to allow this would be terrible for one’s health. Suggesting a number of hours allowed to play will allow people who wish to play casual possible and restrict players who spend more than 12+ hours a day. Also putting a notice within the game to show how long a person is playing can effect what people think about when they are in game. According to Dr. John M. Grohol it is important to “ Track your video game use, to help better control the amount of time spent playing” (Psychcentral 2007). If many video game companies were to put in place these limits it would not only provide a more positive outlook for video gamers it would also protect people who are obsessive over a game. League of Legends would be a great start to this as it is one of the largest online game played today. Another possible proposal is to introduce classes for parents to help prevent video game addiction to young kids. Most of the community that face video game addiction are kids who have plenty of free time and waste it on video games. So it is important for the adult who control the kids to limit their time playing games. It is also needed to have parents help children with their social abilities and let the go outside and get interaction with other kids. Having a good base for students playing video games is really important as these habits will help them control their addiction. With these rules in place it is possible to reduce the number of video gaming addictions and reduce the number of injuries related to playing games.
